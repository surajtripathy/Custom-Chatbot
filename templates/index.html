About

Della is a personalised chatbot for DELL. It is made with the aim of driving Customer engagement.

The main objective was to balance and maintain a healthy relationship between humans and bots. But there's more to it than simply employing a new technology. The secret is delivering a superlative customer experience over and over again. After all, people matter
